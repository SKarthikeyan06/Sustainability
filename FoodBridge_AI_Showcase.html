What Makes Us Different
Innovation & Uniqueness
🧠
Menu-Aware Prediction
Not just quantity — we factor in menu type. Biryani days vs Dal Rice days have different waste rates. Our model learns this pattern from real TN food data.
⚡
Zero Friction for NGOs
NGOs are under-resourced. One notification, one tap to confirm. No app to install, no calls, no forms. Lowest barrier to participation ever designed.
🔄
Three-Phase Prediction
Dataset baseline → Formula calculation → OpenAI prompt refinement. Each phase improves accuracy. Model gets smarter every day with real feedback.
🚨
Public Humanitarian Alert
Anyone who spots a needy person can report their location instantly — no login needed. Nearest NGO is alerted in real time. No other food system has this.

Feasibility & Viability
Can This Actually Work?
Feasibility of the Idea
  • Already built and deployed — live at food-wastage.onrender.com, not just an idea
  • Supabase, Python and Node.js are free and beginner-friendly tools any student can maintain
Potential Challenges & Risks
  • New canteens have no past data, so early predictions may not be accurate
  • NGOs may not always respond in time and food could expire before pickup
Strategies to Overcome
  • Use Tamil Nadu food surplus dataset as baseline until canteen builds its own history
  • If first NGO doesn't confirm in 15 minutes, system automatically alerts the next nearest one
